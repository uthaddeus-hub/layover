Dashboard loads with 5 real trips from the DB.
Point to the trip cards — Miami, Tokyo, London. Show the vibe tags and countdown. Say: "Every trip Alex posts is visible to friends they've connected with."
45 seconds
3
Click the Tokyo trip → trip detail page.
Show the itinerary, friend overlaps panel, and recommendations. This is where the "aha" moment happens — "Two of Alex's friends are also going to Tokyo. Layover caught it automatically."
⚡ Key moment
4
Navigate to Alerts tab.
Show the overlap notifications — "Alex Chen is in Newark at the same time as you." This is the core product moment. The app did the work so the user didn't have to.
⚡ Core value prop
5
Navigate to Explore → click Tokyo.
Show city page: who's there, friend tips, weather, best time to visit. "This is the social layer on top of travel — your friends' knowledge, organized by city."
45 seconds

Turn on location sharing. Show the green dot and live city detection. "Opt-in. Off by default. When it's on, your friends see your city in real time." Then hand the phone back.
