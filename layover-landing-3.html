next reunion.

What's Inside

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Real-time overlap alerts
Instant notifications when a friend's trip overlaps yours — by city, by date, down to the hour.
Trip privacy controls
Share with everyone, close friends only, or keep it entirely private. You're always in control.
Ephemeral trip chat
Temporary group chats that auto-expire when your trip ends. No clutter, no history — just the moment.
City pages & tips
See where your friends have been and what they loved. Crowdsourced insider knowledge from people you trust.
